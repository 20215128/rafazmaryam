About RAFAZ

Building a legacy of excellence in commercial printing since 1988

Our Story

Founded in 1988, RAFAZ began as a small printing shop with a big vision: to deliver uncompromising quality and service to businesses of all sizes. Over the past 25+ years, we've grown into a comprehensive commercial printing facility with 12+ specialized departments and state-of-the-art equipment.

What started with a single offset press has evolved into a full-service printing powerhouse. Today, we serve thousands of clients across multiple industries and countries, from small businesses to Fortune 500 companies, delivering over 50,000 projects annually.

Our success is built on three pillars: cutting-edge technology, skilled craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Every project, regardless of size, receives the same attention to detail and dedication to excellence.
