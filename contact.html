How quickly can I get a quote?

Via WhatsApp — within minutes. Via email — within 24 hours. For complex projects, we may need 1–2 business days for a detailed estimate.

What file formats do you accept?

PDF (preferred), AI, EPS, PSD, CDR, and most common formats. Files must be at 300 DPI minimum with bleeds included.

Do you offer same-day printing?

Yes, for certain job types with prior arrangement. WhatsApp us early in the morning and we'll confirm availability.

Do you deliver outside Jos?

Yes. We offer nationwide delivery via trusted courier partners. Free pickup and delivery within Jos metro for qualifying orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfers, online payments, and cash at our Jos showroom. We issue receipts for all transactions.

Can you handle large corporate orders?

Absolutely. We regularly handle large-volume orders for corporations, government agencies, and institutions. Volume discounts available.
